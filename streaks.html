Streak Records

consecutive top finishes — who's on a heater?
A streak = consecutive COTD cups a player attended finishing at or above the cutline. Cups they sat out are skipped (they don't break it); a finish past the line breaks it. Regular COTD only (no Troll/Roulette). Red rows are still active. Top streaks ranks every run ever — a player can appear more than once; One per player keeps each player's single longest.
